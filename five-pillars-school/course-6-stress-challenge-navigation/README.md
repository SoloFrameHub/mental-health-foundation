## Course Structure

### 5 Modules | 20 Lessons | ~15 Hours Total

1. **Module 1: Understanding Stress Fundamentals** (Lessons 1-4)
   - Stress biology and response systems
   - Personal trigger identification
   - Eustress vs. distress spectrum
   - Physical resilience foundation

2. **Module 2: Cognitive & Interpersonal Techniques** (Lessons 5-10)
   - Cognitive reframing
   - Mindfulness practices
   - Time management
   - Communication skills
   - Support networks
   - Problem-solving strategies

3. **Module 3: Physical Health & Lifestyle** (Lessons 11-15)
   - Relaxation techniques
   - Exercise and physical activity
   - Nutrition for resilience
   - Sleep hygiene
   - Digital wellness

4. **Module 4: Domain-Specific Stress** (Lessons 16-18)
   - Workplace stress management
   - Financial stress and money mindset
   - Relationship conflict resolution

5. **Module 5: Integration & Maintenance** (Lessons 19-20)
   - Personal stress management plan
   - Long-term resilience sustainability
