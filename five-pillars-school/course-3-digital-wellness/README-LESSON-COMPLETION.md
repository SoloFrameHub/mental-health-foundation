## Content Guidelines for Each Lesson

1. **Introduction** (3 paragraphs minimum)
   - Opening hook addressing the topic
   - Research-backed statistics and studies
   - What learners will accomplish in the lesson

2. **Learning Objectives** (4-5 specific goals)

3. **Research Foundation** (1 detailed paragraph citing specific studies)

4. **Insight Cards** (4 cards with icons and key concepts)

5. **Interactive Element**
   - Assessment/quiz with 5-10 questions
   - OR Interactive simulation
   - Must have JavaScript for scoring/feedback

6. **Detailed Content Sections** (3-5 major sections)
   - Each with brain-systems divs
   - Real-world examples
   - Neuroscience explanations
   - Practical applications

7. **Practice Exercises** (3 levels)
   - Beginner (simple, 1-day trial)
   - Intermediate (7-day practice)
   - Advanced (2+ week implementation)

8. **Key Takeaways** (3 sections in recommendation-details grid)

9. **Navigation Footer**
   - Previous/Next lesson buttons
   - Progress indicator
