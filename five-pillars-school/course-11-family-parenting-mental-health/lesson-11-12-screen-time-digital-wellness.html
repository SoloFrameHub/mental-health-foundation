Create healthy technology boundaries that protect mental health while embracing digital benefits

Digital Wellness

Technology impacts family mental health. Excessive screen time correlates with increased anxiety, depression, sleep disturbances, and attention difficulties. Yet thoughtful technology use enhances learning, creativity, and connection. The key is establishing boundaries that maximize benefits while minimizing harm.

Research Foundation

Families implementing structured screen time guidelines demonstrate better mental health outcomes and stronger relationships. Co-viewing and discussing digital content enhances educational value while reducing negative impacts.

üìã Creating a Family Media Plan

Tech-Free Zones & Times

Bedrooms (promotes sleep), mealtimes (enables connection), first/last hour of day, family activities

Age-Appropriate Limits

Under 2: avoid screens; 2-5: 1 hour quality content; 6+: consistent limits balancing activities

Content Quality Matters

Prioritize educational/creative content, co-view when possible, discuss what they're watching, know their online world

Model Healthy Use

Parents' technology habits set the standard. Practice what you preach about screen boundaries.
