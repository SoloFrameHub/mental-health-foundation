Understand trauma responses and create healing environments for children who've experienced adversity

Trauma-informed parenting recognizes behavioral challenges as trauma responses. Many behaviors reflect adaptive survival responses rather than willful misbehavior. Studies show 40% greater improvement when parents understand trauma compared to traditional behavior management.

Trauma impacts brain development, stress systems, and attachment. Healing occurs through safe, predictable relationships making parent-child bonds powerful recovery vehicles.
