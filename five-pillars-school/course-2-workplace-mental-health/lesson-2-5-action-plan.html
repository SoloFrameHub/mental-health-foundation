üìÖ Your Week-by-Week Plan

Here's exactly what to focus on each week:

Week 1

Zone Assessment & Awareness

Focus: Understanding your stress-challenge patterns

üìù Daily Practices (15 min):

  • Morning: Rate your current zone (Active Learning, High Strain, etc.) on a scale of 1-10
  • During work: Track what tasks energize vs. drain you - write down 2-3 examples
  • Evening: Note how long it took to "recover" from work today (30 min? 3 hours? All night?)

üéØ Week 1 Goal:

By the end of Week 1, you should know EXACTLY which situations put you in which zone. No more guessing.

Week 2

Control Enhancement

Focus: Implementing your chosen control strategies

üìù Daily Practices (20 min):

  • Pick ONE strategy from Lesson 2.4 to practice each day (rotate through your 2-3 chosen strategies)
  • Morning: Plan where you'll apply the strategy today - be specific
  • During work: Execute your strategy at least once
  • Evening: Journal what happened - Did it work? What would you adjust?

üéØ Week 2 Goal:

Practice each of your chosen strategies at least 2-3 times. Some will work better than others - that's the point of testing.

Week 3

Support System Building

Focus: You can't increase control alone - build your team

üìù Daily Practices (25 min):

  • Identify 3-5 people who could support your control-building (colleagues, manager, mentor, friends)
  • Each day: Have ONE conversation about your strategies - ask for feedback or support
  • Practice collaborative problem-solving: Bring a challenge to someone and work through it together
  • Give support to others: Help a colleague with their challenge (builds reciprocity)

üéØ Week 3 Goal:

Build a support network that makes increasing control EASIER. Social support is one of the 6 factors - don't skip this.

Week 4

Sustainable Systems

Focus: Making control-building automatic

üìù Daily Practices (30 min):

  • Review your Week 1 assessment: Retake the 6-factor assessment from Lesson 2.3 - compare scores
  • Identify your top 2 strategies that worked best - these become your "default moves"
  • Create triggers: "When X happens (e.g., tight deadline), I will do Y strategy (e.g., boundary negotiation)"
  • Build a recovery protocol: What will you do when you slip back into High Strain?

üéØ Week 4 Goal:

Turn conscious practice into unconscious habit. You should be using at least 1-2 control strategies WITHOUT thinking about it.

  • Aim for 5/7 days per week - perfectionism kills progress

  • Celebrate weekly wins - treat yourself when you complete a week
  • If a week feels too easy, you can speed up. If too hard, slow down or repeat.
