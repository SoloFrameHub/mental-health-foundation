Community Guidelines

Be Respectful & Supportive

Treat all community members with kindness and respect. We're all on a journey toward better mental health.

Protect Privacy

Never share personal information about yourself or others. Keep discussions general and focused on learning.

Not Medical Advice

This forum is for educational purposes only. Always consult healthcare professionals for medical advice.

Share Constructively

Share your experiences, ask questions, and offer support. Keep discussions constructive and evidence-based.

Crisis Resources
