‚öñÔ∏è Balanced Measurement Principles

Guidelines for healthy progress tracking:

  • Process over Outcome: Track effort and learning, not just results
  • Trends over Points: Focus on patterns rather than single measurements
  • Multiple Timeframes: Include daily, weekly, monthly, and yearly perspectives
  • Qualitative Context: Interpret numbers within life circumstances
  • Growth over Performance: Measure improvement rather than absolute levels
  • Intrinsic over Extrinsic: Prioritize personal satisfaction over external validation
