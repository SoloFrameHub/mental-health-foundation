üëë Growth Leadership Principles

Core beliefs and practices of developmental leaders:

  • Potential Over Performance: See capabilities that can be developed, not just current abilities
  • Development Over Evaluation: Focus on growth rather than judgment
  • Questions Over Answers: Guide discovery rather than providing solutions
  • Process Over Outcome: Value learning and effort alongside results
  • Vulnerability Over Perfection: Model continuous learning and growth
  • Systems Over Individuals: Create environments that support everyone's development
