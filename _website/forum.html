Community Guidelines

Creating a safe, supportive space for everyone

Be Respectful & Supportive

Treat all members with kindness, empathy, and respect. Everyone's journey is unique and valid.

Maintain Privacy

Respect confidentiality. Don't share others' personal information or experiences outside the forum.

Stay Safe

If you're in crisis, please contact emergency services. The forum is for peer support, not crisis intervention.

Share Constructively

Offer support based on personal experience. Avoid giving medical advice or diagnosing others.

Report Concerns

Use the report feature if you see content that violates guidelines. Our moderators review all reports promptly.

Stay Positive

Focus on constructive, supportive discussions. Avoid negativity, judgment, or harmful content.
