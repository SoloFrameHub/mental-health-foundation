Essential Course Tool

Your Course Companion Notebook

This isn't just a course you watch—it's an experience you actively engage with. Your personalized notebook is where transformation happens.

What You'll Use It For:

  • Guided Reflections – Prompts appear throughout lessons to help you process and apply concepts
  • Practice Worksheets – Interactive exercises to build and strengthen new skills
  • Progress Tracking – Check-ins, assessments, and milestone reviews show your growth
  • Creative Tools – Rich text editor, drawing canvas, mood scales, and habit trackers
Completely Private

All notebook entries are stored locally on your device—completely private by default. You decide what (if anything) to share in community forums. Export your reflections as PDF anytime for personal records or to share with your therapist.
